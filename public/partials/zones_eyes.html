Eyebrow / Forehead Lift

Brow lift surgery, also referred to as a forehead or eyebrow lift, uses tiny incisions along the hairline to allow the patient to achieve a younger, more refreshed look by smoothing the forehead, reducing frown lines, and elevating the position of the eyebrow line. The brow lift will make you appear years younger.

What to expect following surgery:
  • Swelling with possible bruising
  • Discomfort controlled with medication
  • Minimal incisions
Results
  • A more youthful and refreshed appearance
* Not offered at all Y LIFT Provider locations.

Eyelid Rejuvination

Laser eyelid surgery brings about a fresh, rejuvenated appearance by reducing or even eliminating saggy, puffy skin around the eyes. The results of laser blepharoplasty are usually long-lasting and may even be permanent for some patients who lead a healthy lifestyle.

What to expect following surgery:
  • Bruising and puffiness for 3 to 5 days
  • Discomfort minimal, but controlled with medication if needed
Results
  • Youthful, more refreshed appearance
