The Y EYE® is a brow lift that lifts the brow, adds shape to the eyes and adds fullness to a hollow temple.

The entire Y EYE® procedure takes between 10 - 15 minutes, delivers instant results and lasts between 2 to 4 years, depending on the product used. The patient can resume most daily activities immediately.

Requires no incisions or general anesthesia.

What to expect following the Y EYE® procedure:
  • Immediate, long lasting results
  • Minimal to no bruising or swelling
