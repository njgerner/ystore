Network

Y LIFT® represents the leading edge in facial aesthetic contouring and cosmetic laser skin treatments. Our national network of Y LIFT® trained physicians is committed to providing the best results and an all-around exceptional patient experience with the highest level of care possible.


Y LIFT® offers the latest medical technology; your Y LIFT® procedure is performed in accredited surgical facilities under the care of board certified physicians who undergo specialized training developed by Dr. Yan Trokel, the innovator of the Y LIFT® technique.


The Y LIFT® network extends beyond specialized training. We are committed to ensuring that our physicians and their patients are up to date on the latest advances science and medicine have to offer. Through our continuous improvement methodology we are constantly innovating and updating the services and products available to our network providers.


Y LIFT® Network physicians provide patients the luxury of natural looking results coupled with a rapid recovery. With each individual case, your doctor will devote his/her expertise, taking into account your individual concerns to produce results without the obvious signs of surgery. The Y LIFT® approach is subtle yet transformative!

Completion of Y LIFT training entitles network providers to unique Y LIFT benefits, such as:

  • Reduced in-network pricing
  • Access to marketing materials
  • Y LIFT Optimization Strategies and Services
  • Access to online Y LIFT portal, including discussion forums, best practices, and convenient online ordering
  • Y LIFT, LLC is continuously seeking novel and effective products to add to our offering. In addition, providers can recommend desired products/services to be added to the in-network offering.

Upon registering with the Y LIFT® Network, you will receive our Comprehensive 2-Day Y LIFT® Instructional Course: 2-Day Course with Hands-on Training.

Learn the basics of the Y LIFT® procedure, enhance knowledge of the facial aesthetics, structural volumizing of the face, learn to incorporate the Y LIFT® into your practice, and increase overall comprehension of the performance of JUVÉDERM® and other cosmetic facial fillers. During the second day of the course, learn to conduct a successful Y LIFT® follow up appointment, and how to handle procedural complications
