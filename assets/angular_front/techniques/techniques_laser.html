Laser Skin Rejuvenation

Laser Skin Rejuvenation and Photo Facial treatments leads to tissue contraction, which tighten the skin and improve collagen and correct aged and damaged skin. These treatments correct the outer layers of the skin’s surface to result in a dramatically healthier and younger looking skin.

Because laser treatments treat a portion of the skin’s surface and leaves the surrounding areas intact, it allows for rapid healing and is safe for treating delicate areas such as the neck and chest.

Benefits of laser treatments:
  • Improves large pores, redness, acne and scarring
  • Corrects sun spots, poor skin texture and uneven pigmentation
  • Significantly improves depression in the skin and the appearance of stretch marks
  • Signs of aging, including: jowl and neck laxity, sagginess, under the eyes and nasolabial folds
  • Treats spider veins, age spots and sun-damaged skin.
  • Tones and tightens while improving skin texture and elasticity
