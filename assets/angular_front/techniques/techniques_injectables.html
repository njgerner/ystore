Injectables

Recent advancements in injectables enable them to be used as an alternative to surgery, to immediately add volume to the area and create a more youthful appearance.

These new products enable the modification of facial volumes, such as redefining the jaw line and cheeks, correcting wrinkles and folds and restoring the hands to a more youthful appearance without surgery.

The area to be treated along with the desired result will determine which of the many available injectables is right for you.

We offer the following injectables:
  • A strong and sharply defined mandibular angle – this is the angle that forms the rear of the face shape.
  • A long, strong body of the mandible forming a near perfect straight line – this is the base of the face shape and the very foundation of a youthful face.
  • A prominent chin, which can be squared off or slightly elevated, serving as the front-facing portion of the facial structure.
  • Tracing down the sharp imaginary line from the high point of the cheek (located near the temple region) to the angle of the mandible (starting point).
