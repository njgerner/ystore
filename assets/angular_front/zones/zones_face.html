Neck Lift

The neck lift improves the appearance of the neck area, enhances the jawline, using a small incision under the chin and / or behind the ears. Certain cases may require liposuction to achieve a more youthful neckline.

What to expect following surgery:

  • Swelling and bruising
  • Discomfort controlled with medication
  • Smooth, firmer neck and jawline
